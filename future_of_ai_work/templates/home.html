Welcome to Hack and Viz 2.0

This hackathon unites forward-thinkers to reimagine the future of careers using Artificial Intelligence. Team HackTitans presents a powerful suite of tools to personalize, optimize, and transform how job seekers interact with opportunities and career guidance.

AI Recommendation System

Our AI-powered recommendation system helps job seekers find the most relevant opportunities based on their skills, preferences, and career goals. Using machine learning, it analyzes profiles, job descriptions, and more to match users with the best-fit roles.

Get AI Recommendations

AI-Powered Job Search with Remote Opportunities

This feature enables users to explore remote job openings across various fields. It leverages AI to fetch real-time listings and filters them based on your profile, skills, and preferences.

View Remote Job Listings

AI Mentor for Interview Preparation

Ek AI mentor create karein jo interview preparation ke liye mock interviews de sake. Yeh mentor users ko feedback dega aur unki interview performance ko improve karne ke liye suggestions dega. This interactive tool analyzes answers and gives personalized suggestions using NLP and AI techniques.

Start Interview Practice

Personality-Based Career Recommendations

Discover career paths that align with your personality traits. This tool helps users who may not know about MBTI types like INFP or ESTJ by offering easy-to-understand descriptions. Just choose the one that matches you best, and get a tailored career suggestion based on AI analysis.

Find Your Ideal Career
